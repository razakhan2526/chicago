Event Sourcing and Design:

Creating meaningful connections and business development through events. It is easier said than done and our team is here to support our clients continue success to impress. From tailored event buzz through corporate messaging to creative event design teamed with seamless operation. We are the team who makes event magic happen.

Meeting Management

Our meeting services allow you to focus while Chicago Is… acts as an extension of your team. We take lead on the logistics so you can focus on the attendees, ROI and those VIPs. Or maybe you are looking for a unique meeting space – we are your team for your individual program needs!

Design and Branding:

Elevating your event to the next level through design and branding is our favorite piece of the perfect event puzzle. From meeting enhancement, tradeshow booth design to immersive event experiences we the solution to make your event dreams a reality. Having trouble finding inspiration? Our team will run with the creative to provide custom one of kind solutions your guests have never seen before.

Transportation:

The first guest experience starts with transportation, and we do not take this job lightly. Our team is full of logistics experience and know the importance of the arrival. Let us take the headache out of the airport, the mess out of city traffic and sprinkle our teams magic on your manifest. We will whisk your guests away on their journey with a smile and deliver them safely to the next destination.

Tours:

Whether it’s a break from the meeting, a chance to network or one-of-a-kind experience. Our team has the insight to create a seamlessly operated tour program. With so many sights to see in Chicago we will optimize your selection to create memories for groups while showcasing Chicago.
