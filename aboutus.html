About Us

We Deliver the Goods that Hype is Made Of

Chicago Is… began over 40 years ago as one of the first Destination Management Companies. The Founding Owner recognized that large City-Wide Conventions needed additional support and services. Over the decades Chicago Is… continued to be the top choice for Full Event Production in Corporate, Association and Incentive Programs. While the pandemic changed how many businesses function our team continued to offer boutique solutions to our clients during unprecedented times. Employee of ten years, Katherine Kirk Fenech, is now at the helm with lofty goals to drive the business forward and continue the company’s innovative foundation.

Our approach is unique because our team is one of a kind. While we act as an extension of your team, utilizing our destination expertise paired with exclusive vendor relationships. This combination creates matchless solutions for your program’s individual needs. Our team is here to make every dollar count and help manage the budget while providing unparalleled event solutions.
